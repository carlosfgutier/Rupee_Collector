Rupee Collector

Ganon took over Hyrule again. Zelda is not happy and you need to give her flowers, but Ganon burned all the ones around the castle. Gerudo is a desert and the Zora don't really have anything but seaweeds, so a Goron flowerbomb it is!


The Goron florist is picky. He will only sell you the flower if you give him the exact amount of rupees.


Find and click on the rupees scattered around the field to match the price, win, and save the kingdom. Fail to match the exact amount and no flower for Zelda. Good Luck!
